Decision Tree for Classification¶

Use bill_authentication.csv Preview the documentto predict whether a bank note is authentic or fake depending upon the four different attributes of the image of the note. The attributes are Variance of wavelet transformed image, curtosis of the image, entropy, and skewness of the image.

The rest of the steps to implement this algorithm in Scikit-Learn are identical to any typical machine learning problem, we will import libraries and datasets, perform some data analysis, divide the data into training and testing sets, train the algorithm, make predictions, and finally we will evaluate the algorithm's performance on our dataset

Summary¶

Tree based models split the data multiple times according to certain cutoff values in the features. Through splitting, different subsets of the dataset are created, with each instance belonging to one subset. The final subsets are called terminal or leaf nodes and the intermediate subsets are called internal nodes or split nodes. To predict the outcome in each leaf node, the average outcome of the training data in this node is used. Trees can be used for classification and regression.

Tree interpretation¶

we have 5 features among them 4 are labels('Variance','Skewness','Curtosis','Entropy') and 1 is target variable(Class).

Classification tree the root of the tree is selected with the gini value calculated with neighbour features and which ever is having max information gain is considered as root.

Feature variance <=0.312 and gini value 0.49 and no of samples of records 1029 and values between 580 and 449

**condition variance is less then or equal to 0.312 it will send to the left side True to the Skewness as decision box with <= 7.56 is again two childs(decison branch) variance with <=-0.404 , gini value 0.136 and variance with <=-4.72 ,gini value =0.28 ,no of samples 83 into 2 leafs gini = 0 left sample have 14 rows and value[0,14] and right leaf sample rows have value[69,0].

**Variance in second branch variance <=-0.403 is divided into 2 subsclasses with Curtosis <=6.74 and Curtosis <=0.441 with samples 348 and 61 , gini values 0.072 and 0.402 .Two curtosis each have 2 leafs left curtosis left branch have gini 0.008 and 0.213 with samples 249 and 99.Right Decision branch Curtosis with gini 0.097 and 0.43 with samples 39 and 22.

**From root branch with false decision Curtosis <=-4.443 with gini 0.187 with samples 537 is divided into 2 more decison branches right variance <=1.59 and left variance <=3.205 and left Variance having gini value 0.336 of samples 29 is divided into leafs with gini values 0 and left leaf have 22 samples with value [0,22] and right leaf value [7,0]

**From the right variance <=1.59 with gini 0.125 of samples 508 have 2 more decison branches left Curtosis and right Variance ,left Curtosis have gini value 0.359 with samples 141 divided into 2 leafs ,left leaf gini = 0.255 and samples 20 and value [3,17] and right decision branch Variance <=2.018 with gini 0.005 and no of samples 367 is divided into 2 leafs left and right ,left leaf with gini =.049 and sample 40 and value=[39,1] and right leaf with gini 0.0and samples 327 and values [327,0]

In short, this measure ranging from 0 to 1, shows how well is the classification model is performing in general, where the higher the number the better.
